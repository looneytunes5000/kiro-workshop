# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Software developers, engineers, and non-technical learners who want to understand AI-native development using TRAE SOLO. They arrive seeking hands-on learning through real exercises, not theory. The interface should feel welcoming to beginners while maintaining enough technical credibility for experienced engineers.

## Product Purpose

A workshop documentation site that teaches AI-driven development through guided exercises. It exists to help learners quickly navigate from concept to hands-on practice. Success means learners can follow the material end-to-end without frustration, find what they need, and retain confidence in the tooling.

## Brand Personality

Professional yet approachable. Confident without being corporate. Clear without being clinical. The three words: **polished, practical, modern.** Emotional goals: confidence in the tool, clarity of the path, calm focus while learning.

## Anti-references

- Corporate enterprise templates with generic stock imagery and dry formatting
- Playful or childish interfaces with cartoonish elements, excessive illustration, or casual styling
- Dense wall-of-text documentation layouts with poor visual hierarchy and overwhelming information density

## Design Principles

**Show, don't tell.** Let visual examples and code speak louder than explanation.

**Hierarchy before decoration.** Structure and spacing carry meaning; decoration is noise.

**Progressive clarity.** Surface the most important information first, let details unfold on demand.

**Respect the learner's time.** Every interaction should move them forward, never sideways.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA standards. Support reduced motion preferences. Ensure color contrast meets AA requirements for all text. Provide keyboard navigation for all interactive elements.
