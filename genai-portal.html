GenAI Portal

Web-based gateway for LLM, image and video generation models

What is the GenAI Portal?

The GenAI Portal is a web-based platform that provides contestants with access to a range of advanced generative AI models through a unified interface. Instead of managing multiple accounts and tools, you can use one portal to generate text, images, and videos — all optimised for hackathon projects.

Additional Credits

The GenAI Portal provides you with extra credits to accelerate your hackathon development:

  • LLM Tokens: 1,000,000
  • Image Generation: 100 images
  • Video Generation: 30 videos
  • Deep Research: 30 runs
aiHackathon Notice

The GenAI Portal is available for contestants to use during the aiHackathon. Extra tokens are provided for contestants to accelerate development and create richer project demos.
