Ollama

Run AI models locally on your machine

What is Ollama?

Ollama is a tool that lets you run large language models (LLMs) locally on your own machine. Instead of relying on cloud services or paid APIs, you can download and run open-source models directly — giving you full control over your AI development workflow during the hackathon.

Key Capabilities

  • Run AI on your own computer — Download smart AI models and use them right on your laptop or desktop — no internet needed
  • Easy one-line commands — Start any model with a simple text command, like telling it "open up"
  • Works with popular models — Access thousands of free AI models from places like Hugging Face without any complicated setup
  • Built-in connection for your projects — Your AI coding tools can talk to Ollama as if it were a cloud service, but everything stays on your machine
  • Pick the right brain for the job — Use a coding-focused model for writing code, or switch to a creative model for writing content — your choice
  • Use as much as you want — No limits, no paywalls, no "you've used up your credits" — run your model as many times as you like
aiHackathon Resource

Ollama is available for all aiHackathon contestants. Use it whenever your solution requires AI model integration but you don't want to rely on cloud-based APIs. All models run locally on your machine.

Sample Use Case

Suppose you're building a web application that needs to summarise user-submitted text on the spot. Instead of connecting to a paid API, you can ask your AI coding agent in TRAE or OpenCode to integrate Ollama directly into your project.
