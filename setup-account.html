Setting Up Your TRAE SOLO Account

A TRAE SOLO account gives you access to all AI-powered development features, cloud agent capabilities, and your personalized development environment. In this step, you'll create or sign in to your account.

Why You Need an Account

Your TRAE SOLO account enables:

  • AI-powered code generation and assistance
  • Cloud agent for background task execution
  • Personalized settings and preferences
  • Access to MCP integrations and extensions

Creating an Account

  1. Sign up with email or SSO

    Visit the TRAE SOLO sign-up page and create an account using your email address or your organization's single sign-on (SSO) provider.

  2. Choose your plan

    Select the plan that fits your needs. TRAE SOLO offers free and paid tiers with varying levels of AI usage and features.

  3. Access web or desktop client

    Once your account is created, you can access TRAE SOLO through the web client or sign in on the desktop client.

Signing In

Web Client

  1. Navigate to the TRAE SOLO web application
  2. Click "Sign In" and enter your credentials
  3. Complete any two-factor authentication if enabled
  4. You'll be redirected to your TRAE SOLO workspace

Desktop Client

  1. Open the TRAE SOLO Desktop application
  2. Click "Sign In" on the welcome screen or in the account settings
  3. A browser window will open for authentication
  4. Enter your credentials and authorize the desktop app
  5. Return to the desktop app - you should now be authenticated

Account Settings

After signing in, you can configure your account settings:

  • Profile preferences - Update your display name, avatar, and notification settings
  • API keys - Generate and manage API keys for integrations and external tools
  • Usage monitoring - Track your AI usage, quota, and billing information
Important Notes
  • Free tier available: TRAE SOLO offers a free tier with generous AI usage limits
  • One account, multiple clients: Your account works across both web and desktop clients
  • Secure authentication: TRAE SOLO supports email, SSO, and two-factor authentication

Verify Authentication

To verify that your account is set up correctly:

  1. Open the TRAE SOLO AI chat panel
  2. Type a simple question like "Hello, are you working?"
  3. Verify that you receive a response from the AI
  4. Check the status indicator in the AI panel - it should show "Connected" or similar

Troubleshooting

If you encounter authentication issues:

  • Browser doesn't open: Check your default browser settings and ensure pop-ups aren't blocked
  • Authorization fails: Make sure you're using the correct credentials and your account is active
  • AI features still unavailable: Try restarting TRAE SOLO after authentication
  • Session expires: You may need to re-authenticate periodically - simply repeat the sign-in process
  • Network issues: Ensure you have a stable internet connection, as AI features require cloud connectivity

You're Ready!

Congratulations! You've completed the setup phase. You now have:

  • TRAE SOLO installed and configured (or web access ready)
  • Your account set up and authenticated
  • AI features enabled and working
